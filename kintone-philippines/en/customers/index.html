Travelbook Philippines Inc.

30–50 sheets of paper per day, per employee — reduced to zero. Staff can finally leave the office.

✈️ Travel & Hospitality 📍 Philippines 👥 126 Employees
The Challenge

Paper-based approvals held the whole company hostage — someone always had to be in the office

  • 30–50 paper submissions per employee per day requiring in-person signatures — impossible to leave the office
  • Purchase paperwork, business trip requests, refund approvals — all required physical presence
  • Audits were chaotic — finding the right form from thousands of paper files took hours, and files were sometimes lost entirely
  • Staff communication was siloed — no way to attach context to approvals or track decision history
The Kintone Solution

One local staff member built and maintains the entire system — starting with call centers and accounting

  • All 60+ admin processes digitized by one employee familiar with Excel — no technical training required
  • Digital approvals with full comment threads — context stays attached to every decision permanently
  • Audit-ready by design — approval history built into every record, searchable in seconds
  • Expanded from call center pilot to company-wide in under 6 months
