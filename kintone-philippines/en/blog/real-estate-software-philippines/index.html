😤 Common Challenges in Real Estate Software

Philippine real estate software companies face unique operational challenges that generic software can't solve. Here's what we hear from businesses every day:

Leads Falling Through Cracks

Inquiries from Lamudi, Property24, Facebook, and walk-ins scattered across platforms. No systematic follow-up means lost sales opportunities worth millions.

Manual Commission Tracking

Brokers and agents tracking commissions in personal spreadsheets. Disputes over splits, delayed payouts, and no visibility into pipeline value.

Document Chaos

Contracts, titles, tax declarations, and permits stored in filing cabinets and random folders. Finding a specific document takes hours.

No Portfolio Overview

Property developers managing multiple projects with no unified view of inventory, availability, pricing, and reservation status across all developments.
