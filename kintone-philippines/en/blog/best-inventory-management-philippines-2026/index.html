📦 What to Look for in Inventory Software

Philippine businesses should prioritize these features when choosing inventory software: real-time stock tracking across multiple locations, purchase order management with approval workflows, supplier management and lead time tracking, barcode/QR code scanning support, integration with accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero), mobile access for warehouse staff, and automated reorder point alerts.

Kintone covers all of these features through customizable apps, with the advantage that you can extend the system to include CRM, HR, and project management without buying separate software.

📍 Warehouse and Multi-Location Management

Philippine businesses operating across multiple warehouses or branch locations face a unique challenge: real-time inventory visibility. Many still rely on end-of-day reports or weekly stock counts, leading to stockouts at one location while another has excess inventory.

Kintone's inventory apps provide real-time stock levels across all locations, with inter-location transfer requests that include approval workflows and delivery confirmation. Staff at any branch can check availability at other locations from their mobile device and initiate transfers with a few taps.

For businesses with warehouse operations, Kintone handles receiving, put-away location tracking, pick-pack-ship workflows, and cycle counting — all built as custom apps that match your specific warehouse layout and processes. No expensive WMS (Warehouse Management System) required.

🔗 Integration with Philippine Business Systems

No inventory system operates in isolation. Philippine businesses need their inventory data connected to accounting (for COGS and asset valuation), sales (for demand forecasting), and procurement (for automated reordering). Kintone's REST API and webhook capabilities make these integrations straightforward.

Common Philippine integration scenarios: Kintone inventory synced with QNE or QuickBooks for accounting, Kintone stock levels pushed to Shopify or Lazada for e-commerce availability, and Kintone reorder alerts triggering purchase orders to suppliers via email or supplier portals.

For businesses using barcode or QR code scanning, Kintone's mobile app supports camera-based scanning for receiving, picking, and stock counting — eliminating manual entry errors and dramatically speeding up warehouse operations.
