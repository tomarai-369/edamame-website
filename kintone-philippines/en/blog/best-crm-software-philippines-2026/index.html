How to Choose the Right CRM for Your Philippine Business

When selecting a CRM, consider these factors:

1. Company Size & Growth Plans

  • Startups (1-10 employees): HubSpot Free, Zoho Free, or Freshsales
  • SMEs (10-100 employees): Kintone, Zoho, or Pipedrive
  • Enterprises (100+ employees): Kintone, Salesforce, or Microsoft Dynamics

2. Industry Requirements

  • Manufacturing: Kintone (production tracking, QC, inventory)
  • Real Estate: Zoho CRM or Kintone (listings, leads, contracts)
  • BPO/Call Centers: Salesforce or Kintone (ticketing, SLAs)
  • Retail: HubSpot or Zoho (e-commerce integration)

3. Budget Considerations

  • Free: HubSpot, Zoho (3 users), Bitrix24
  • Under ‚Ç±1,000/user: Zoho, Monday.com, Pipedrive

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best CRM for small business in Philippines?

For small businesses, we recommend starting with HubSpot Free if you have zero budget, or Kintone if you want a customizable platform that grows with your business. Zoho CRM is another solid budget option.

How much does CRM software cost in the Philippines?

CRM pricing ranges from free (HubSpot, Zoho limited) to ‚Ç±5,000+/user/month for enterprise solutions. Most mid-range CRMs like Kintone cost ‚Ç±1,500-3,000/user/month. Always factor in implementation, training, and customization costs.

Which CRM has local support in the Philippines?

Kintone (through Edamame Inc.) offers the most comprehensive local support with English, Filipino, and Japanese language options. Salesforce and Zoho also have local partners or presence.

Can I switch from Excel to CRM?

Yes! Most CRMs allow you to import Excel data. Kintone is particularly good for Excel migration because it can replicate your spreadsheet structure while adding automation, mobile access, and multi-user capabilities.

Is Salesforce worth it for Philippine SMEs?

Salesforce is powerful but often overkill for SMEs. The total cost of ownership (licenses + customization + admin) can be prohibitive. Consider Kintone or Zoho for similar capabilities at lower cost.
