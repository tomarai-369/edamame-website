What a Kintone HR System Covers

1. Employee Records Management

A centralized employee database with all fields relevant to Philippine HR: full name, employee number, TIN, SSS/PhilHealth/Pag-IBIG numbers, emergency contacts, employment date, position, department, salary grade, regularization date, and employment status. Documents (contract, IDs, certificates) attached directly to the employee record.

2. Leave Management

Philippine leave types built in from day one: Vacation Leave, Sick Leave, Service Incentive Leave (SIL), Maternity Leave (105 days), Paternity Leave (7 days), Solo Parent Leave, and company-specific leaves. Employees submit leave requests through Kintone, managers approve digitally, the calendar updates automatically, and HR sees real-time headcount at any given date.

3. Attendance and Timekeeping

Log daily attendance, late arrivals, overtime requests, and schedule deviations. Connect to biometric systems via API or manage manually through the app. Automatic summaries for payroll cut-off periods.

4. Expense and Reimbursement

Employees submit expense reports with uploaded receipts directly in Kintone. Amounts auto-tabulated, routing to the appropriate approver based on amount thresholds and department. Finance sees a dashboard of pending reimbursements by cost center.

5. Performance Management

KPI tracking, quarterly review forms, goal-setting records, and 360-feedback workflows — all in Kintone, with historical data kept permanently and accessible to HR and leadership.

Guhring Philippines built exactly this. Their non-IT sales employee built a leave request app with embedded permissions so employees could file and get approvals without HR needing to manually track anything. The team calendar updates in real time.

Philippine Labor Law Compliance with Kintone

Kintone HR systems can be configured to enforce Philippine labor law requirements automatically:

  • SIL accrual tracking and annual conversion to cash (if not availed)
  • 13th month pay computation support
  • Night differential and overtime records for DOLE audit purposes
  • Regularization date tracking with automatic alerts for probationary end dates
