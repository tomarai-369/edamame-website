🟠 What is HubSpot CRM?

HubSpot CRM is a CRM platform known for its excellent free tier and best-in-class inbound marketing tools. It's particularly popular with content-driven and marketing-focused businesses.

✅ HubSpot Strengths

  • Excellent free CRM tier for small teams
  • Best-in-class inbound marketing tools
  • Beautiful, intuitive interface
  • Strong content management and SEO tools

⚠️ HubSpot Limitations

  • Pricing skyrockets once you outgrow free tier (Professional starts ~₱45,000/month)
  • CRM structure is rigid — limited customization
  • Per-contact pricing means costs scale with your database
  • Marketing Hub required for most automation features
  • No no-code app building beyond CRM

🎯 Which Should You Choose?

Choose HubSpot If:

Your primary need is inbound marketing + basic CRM, you have a small team (under 5) that fits the free tier, you're content-marketing focused, or you need native email marketing sequences.

Choose Kintone If:

You need more than just CRM, want to build custom business apps, your team is growing beyond 5 users (where HubSpot gets expensive), need inventory/HR/project management alongside CRM, or want local PH support.

🎯 When Each Platform Makes Sense

HubSpot is the right choice for Philippine businesses whose primary challenge is lead generation and inbound marketing. If you're a SaaS company, digital agency, or e-commerce brand that needs landing pages, email nurture sequences, blog analytics, and social media scheduling — HubSpot's marketing tools are best-in-class.

Kintone is the right choice for Philippine businesses whose primary challenge is operational efficiency. If you're a manufacturing company, BPO, logistics firm, or professional services company that needs CRM plus custom workflows for procurement, inventory, HR processes, project tracking, and compliance — Kintone gives you everything on one platform without per-module pricing.

Many Philippine businesses actually use both: HubSpot for marketing and lead capture, and Kintone for everything that happens after the lead converts — project delivery, operations, customer service, and internal processes. Kintone's REST API connects cleanly with HubSpot via Zapier or direct integration.

💡 The Hidden Cost of HubSpot's Ecosystem

HubSpot's pricing model is designed around 'hubs' — Marketing Hub, Sales Hub, Service Hub, Operations Hub, and CMS Hub. Each hub has Free, Starter, Professional, and Enterprise tiers. When Philippine businesses need functionality across multiple hubs, costs compound rapidly.

A common scenario: a Philippine company starts on HubSpot Free CRM, adds Marketing Starter ($20/mo), then needs workflow automation (Professional at $890/mo), then needs custom reporting (Enterprise at $3,600/mo). Within 18 months, they're paying more than a full ERP system.

Kintone's pricing is radically simple: one price per user, all features included. Build as many apps as you need — CRM, HR, inventory, project management, compliance tracking — with no per-module fees, no tier upgrades, no contact limits.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is HubSpot free CRM really free in the Philippines?

HubSpot's free CRM tier is genuinely free with up to 1,000,000 contacts. However, it's limited — no custom reporting, no workflow automation, and minimal customization. Most Philippine businesses outgrow it within 6 months and face HubSpot's steep upgrade pricing: Starter at $20/month, Professional at $890/month, and Enterprise at $3,600/month.

Can Kintone do marketing automation like HubSpot?

Kintone is not a marketing automation platform. It's a no-code business app builder. For inbound marketing, email sequences, and landing pages, HubSpot is purpose-built and superior. The question for Philippine businesses is whether you need marketing automation or operational efficiency — most SMEs need the latter first.

Which is better for a Philippine sales team — HubSpot or Kintone?

For pure sales pipeline management with built-in email tracking and meeting scheduling, HubSpot is excellent. For a Philippine sales team that also needs to manage quotations, purchase orders, delivery tracking, and after-sales service, Kintone is more versatile because you can build custom apps for every stage of the customer lifecycle.

How much does HubSpot actually cost for a Philippine business?

HubSpot's pricing escalates dramatically. A 10-user Philippine business on Marketing Hub Professional pays approximately ₱50,000/month ($890 USD). Adding Sales Hub Professional adds another ₱28,000/month. By contrast, Kintone for the same team costs ₱10,000/month total with unlimited custom apps. Over 3 years, the difference exceeds ₱2M.
