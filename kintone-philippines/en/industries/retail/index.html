Common Questions
Everything you want to know before your demo
How long does it take to deploy a Kintone app?
Most first apps are live within 3–7 days of your initial consultation. Complex multi-app systems typically take 2–4 weeks. Compare this to custom development (3–12 months) or ERP implementation (6–18 months).
Do we need technical staff to maintain Kintone?
No. Kintone is designed for non-technical users. Any staff member who understands your business process can build and maintain apps using the drag-and-drop interface. No coding, no IT degree required.
Is Kintone secure for sensitive Philippine business data?
Yes. Kintone uses enterprise-grade security: AES-256 encryption, granular role-based access controls, audit logs for every action, and SOC2 compliance. Data can be hosted in regional data centers.
What does Kintone cost for Philippine businesses?
Kintone pricing is per-user, per-month — significantly more affordable than ERP systems or enterprise software. Contact us for a Philippines-specific quote tailored to your team size and requirements.
Can Kintone integrate with our existing systems?
Yes. Kintone has a full REST API and connects with hundreds of tools including Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack, and custom-built systems. Many Philippine businesses connect Kintone to their existing accounting software.
Can Kintone integrate with our existing POS system?
Yes. Kintone's REST API allows integration with most POS systems. Sales data from your POS can be synced into Kintone for unified inventory management and analytics — without replacing the POS terminal your staff are already familiar with.
