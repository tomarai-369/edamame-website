Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kintone and how is it different from Excel or Google Sheets?

Kintone is a no-code platform that lets you build real business applications—not just spreadsheets. Unlike Excel, Kintone offers: automated workflows, role-based access control, built-in notifications, mobile apps, API integrations, and true database relationships. Your data is secure, organized, and accessible from anywhere.

How much does Kintone cost in the Philippines?

Kintone pricing varies based on the number of users. We offer flexible plans for SMEs to enterprise organizations. Contact us for a free consultation and customized quote in Philippine Pesos (PHP). We also offer free trials so you can test before committing.

How long does it take to implement Kintone?

Most businesses go live within days, not months. Simple apps can be built in hours. Complex enterprise systems typically take 2-4 weeks. Our proven 4-step process (Discovery, Design, Build, Launch) ensures rapid deployment without compromising quality.

Can I integrate Kintone with my existing software?

Yes! Kintone has extensive integration capabilities via REST API and webhooks. We can connect it to your ERP, accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero, SAP), email platforms, Slack, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and 1,000+ other apps. Our team specializes in custom integrations.

Is Kintone secure for sensitive business data?

Absolutely. Kintone provides enterprise-grade security: SOC 2 Type II certified, ISO 27001 compliant, 256-bit encryption, role-based access controls, audit logs, two-factor authentication, and 99.99% uptime. Your data is stored in secure data centers with automatic backups.

Do you provide training and support?

Yes! We provide comprehensive hands-on training for your team and ongoing technical support. As your local Philippine partner, we offer support in English, Filipino, and Japanese. We're available via phone, email, Viber, and Messenger during business hours.
